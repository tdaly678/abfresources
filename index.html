About ABF Ministry

Adult Bible Fellowships at LEFC are Sunday morning small groups that gather around God's Word — studying Scripture, building community, and caring for one another. ABFs are a cornerstone of how LEFC makes and matures disciples. As a lay-led ministry, ABF leaders are trusted to shepherd their groups with consistency and heart, supported by pastoral staff and this resource hub.
